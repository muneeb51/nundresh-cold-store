About Nundresh Cold Store

Our story, our values, and the people behind Kashmir's trusted cold chain

Our Story

25 Years of Cold Chain Excellence

Founded in 1998 in the heart of Kashmir's apple belt, Nundresh Cold Store began as a small facility serving local orchardists in Nundresh village. Over two decades, we have grown into one of the valley's most trusted industrial cold storage providers.

Our growth has always followed the needs of our growers. As apple cultivation expanded and markets demanded longer shelf lives, we invested in controlled atmosphere technology, automated monitoring, and expanded capacity — ensuring that Kashmir's prized fruit reaches domestic and international markets in perfect condition.

Today, we serve over 500 growers and fruit merchants across Sopore, Shopian, Pulwama, and beyond, with a combined storage capacity exceeding 5,000 metric tonnes.

1998
Year Founded

Expanded to 2,000 MT with modern refrigeration (2005)

Meet the Owner

👨‍💼

Mr. Feroz ahmad khan

Founder & Managing Director

A lifelong resident of Kashmir and a passionate advocate for the valley's apple industry, Mr.feroz ahmad khan founded this facility with a vision to empower local growers with the same cold chain infrastructure available in global markets. With over 30 years in the fruit industry, he personally oversees facility operations, ensuring every batch of apples receives the care and precision it deserves.

His deep relationships with growers across Sopore, Shopian, and Pulwama, combined with his understanding of modern cold chain logistics, have made Nundresh Cold Store synonymous with trust in the Kashmir valley.
